Gardening Program

Building confidence, wellness, independence, and community through hands-on gardening experiences for autistic and neurodivergent individuals.

Register Learn More

What We'll Grow

Each week participants will get hands-on experience planting, tending, and harvesting a variety of vegetables, herbs, and flowers. Here's a look at what we'll be cultivating together:

🍅

Tomatoes

Learn to stake, prune, and water tomato plants while watching them ripen from green to red.

🥬

Lettuce

Lettuce — fast-growing and rewarding to harvest fresh from the earth.

🌿

Basil

Learning to interact with growing herbs. Enjoy the sesnsory experience by smelling and tating fresh herbs.

🌻

Sunflowers

A beloved favorite — plant seeds and watch these cheerful giants reach for the sky.

🥒

Cucumbers

Fast-growing vines that teach patience and observation as they climb and sprawl.

🌿

Cilantro

Learning to interact with growing herbs. Enjoy the sesnsory experience by smelling and tating fresh herbs.

🌱 No experience needed. Every session is led by experienced gardeners who will guide participants through each step — from planting seeds to understanding why plants need sunlight, water, and care. All tools and materials are provided.

How We Use Event Funds

Thanks to our partners, sponsors, donors, and participants of this event, we are proud to donate $1,000 to a local business that services the autism community.

Our organization is less than one year old, and this is our first event. This milestone shows that we are committed to supporting our community, and we hope that with each event we will be able to grow our impact and support the autism community even more.

All funds outside of this donation have been used to cover the cost of organizing this event, as well as an initial investment into our next event and the continued growth of the Puzzles organization.

Our goal is to build a sustainable organization that can continue creating events, fostering community, and centering our mission around supporting people with autism and neurodivergent individuals.

We hope you can support this mission and help us grow as we work toward becoming a fully established nonprofit organization that can scale its impact and build a movement that truly matters.

Special thanks to our sponsors and partners!

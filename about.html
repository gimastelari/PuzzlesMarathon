Our Mission

Build a Society That Belongs to Everyone

Our mission is to build a society that is truly inclusive and accessible for people with autism and on the neurodivergent spectrum. We believe that every person deserves to feel seen, celebrated, and supported in their community — not as an afterthought, but by design.

Our Vision

A Summer Camp Like No Other

Every event and program we run brings us one step closer to our ultimate vision — a summer camp purpose-built for autistic, neurodivergent, and disabled individuals. A place with the best programming, the most inclusive environment, and a team that truly understands the community it serves.

That's what we're building toward. Every registration, every donation, every volunteer hour moves us forward.

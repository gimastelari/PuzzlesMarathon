Support

Accessibility Plan

  • Wheelchair‑friendly route and start/finish areas
  • Sensory‑friendly zones (quiet tents, headphones, visual schedules)
  • Clear signage, wayfinding, and volunteer training
  • Accessible restrooms & parking near start/finish
  • Communication access: large‑print maps, alt text, readable colors

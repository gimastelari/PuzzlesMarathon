About the 5K

Puzzles Marathon is an inclusive, city-backed community run supporting the autism community through awareness, accessibility, and connection. Our mission is to create a welcoming space where people of all abilities can come together, move, connect, and celebrate neurodiversity.
This run is officially sanctioned by USA Track & Field and timed by FastTrack Timing.
You don't need to run or be professionally timed to be part of this run, you can walk or jog.

Event Details

  • Date: April 25, 10:00 AM – 2:00 PM
  • Location: Hatters Park — 36 E Hayestown Rd, Danbury, CT
  • Distance: 5K Run / Walk

This event is designed as a festival-style community gathering that brings families, advocates, businesses, and supporters together to celebrate and support the autism community.

  • 🎈 Kids Zone: Bouncy houses, foam pits, and park activities
  • 🌮 Food Trucks & Drinks: Local food vendors and refreshments
  • 🛍 Vendor Village: Local businesses and organizations
  • 🤝 Sponsor Row: Meet the partners supporting the event
  • 🎶 Live Music & Bands: Entertainment throughout the day
  • 🎤 Speakers & Special Guests: Community leaders and advocates
  • 🌅 Lakeside Setting: Enjoy the beautiful park and scenic lake views

Community Impact

Every step taken at Puzzles Marathon helps fund inclusive programming, accessibility initiatives, and community-based organizations.

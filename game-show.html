Why we play

Game night with a purpose.

This event is fundraising for Puzzles Community's main mission: opening a summer camp for the neurodivergent and special-needs community. On top of funding the Puzzles camp, proceeds will also sponsor the full testing, application, and licensing fees for two BCBAs (Board Certified Behavior Analysts) — investing directly in the people who make this community stronger.

Puzzles Community is a Gen Z–led nonprofit building neurodivergent-inclusive spaces — from our gardening program to our annual 5K, and now, our biggest stage yet. The Puzzles Game Show brings teams of friends, families, and coworkers together for a night of friendly competition, real prizes, and a shared reason to show up. It's a chance to keep growing our mission by investing in the individuals, families, organizations, businesses, and initiatives that strengthen and support this community.

Every ticket and sponsorship goes directly toward program costs: sensory-friendly accommodations, staff training, and scholarships that keep our events free or low-cost for the families who need them most.

Good to know

Questions, answered.

Is the venue accessible and sensory-friendly?

Yes. Prodigy Athletic Center is wheelchair accessible, and we offer a quiet room, dimmed seating, and noise-reducing headphones on request — just note it during checkout.

Can I buy tickets for a whole team?

Absolutely — increase the quantity on any ticket tier and add your team name during checkout. Tables seat up to 8.

What's the refund policy?

Tickets are final sale, but fully transferable — email us the new attendee's name anytime before the event.

How do sponsorships get invoiced?

After you submit the sponsor form, our team follows up within 2 business days with an invoice and secure payment link, or ACH/check instructions on request.
